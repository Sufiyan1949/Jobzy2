About Us

What do we do?

We at OKEANTECH have put an collective effort to solve the problems of Students, graduates and Job seekers finding internships and jobs in tech jobs all over India through our website by collecting data and present the available internships and jobs alike over many job search engines on our single job search portal. Users can find featured jobs according to their niche, or can fulfil the ambition of getting their dream jobs on top Multi National Corporations like Google, Amazon, Netflix, Meta, etc. OKEANTECH is also a platform for ongoing University students, fresh graduates to find internships of all types of levels according to their preferences including Government internships, opportunities of lucrative stipends provided by top Companies.

Why are we doing this?

The main purpose of our project is to reduce the irritating inconvenience that a person goes through while looking for jobs and internships browsing through different job search engines which leads to confusion and a wastage of time. Companies also miss out on qualified candidates, If the HR department of a company isn’t competent with the internet, it might lose out on the most valuable and well-trained candidates for the job. This loss of human capital can present massive costs down the line, which will outweigh the initial savings in recruitment costs.

What make us different from others?

We understand that looking for a job can be a daunting task, which is why we've created a platform that makes the process easy and stress-free. Here's what sets us apart from other job portals:

One-Stop Shop: Our website collects information from multiple job portals, so you don't have to visit several sites to find the perfect job. All the information you need is available in one place.

Comprehensive Coverage: Our website covers more job portals than any other site, which means you'll have access to a wider range of job opportunities. We leave no stone unturned in our search for the best job postings.

Simplified User Experience: Our website has a clean, user-friendly interface that makes it easy for you to navigate and find the information you need. You'll spend less time searching for job postings and more time applying for the jobs you want.
